Lisinopril

Lisinopril is the generic drug name for Zestril. It is a blood pressure medication in the angiotensin converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitor group of drugs.

Doctors prescribe lisinopril to treat high blood pressure in adults and children 6 years old and older. People may also take lisinopril with other medications if they have heart failure. Doctors may also prescribe the drug for someone who has experienced a heart attack.
